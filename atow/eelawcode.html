Philippine Electrical Code

The Philippine Electrical Code has played a big role in the Electrical Industry and has contributed to the growth of our economy. Its strength, dynamism, and adaptabillity will allow it to continue its share in building our industry.

The electrical design parameters, electrical installation guidelines and safety rules in the Philippines Electrical Code have been formulated and develop to safeguard persons and property from hazards arising from the use of electricity. In order for this objective to be achieved, the Code must be followed and enforced. Our very own Electrical Practitioners, Building Officials, Electrical Contractors and skilled workers have to perform their duties and responsibilities in conformance with the Philippine Electrical Code. The strict compliance with the Code is a must.

The Philippine Electrical Code through the years has been the most useful tool in the electrical industry. It is the Magna CArta that governs the standards and practices in electrical engineering, design and construction. It guides the electrical designer in the preparation of the electrical plans, speccification and project studies to come up with a complete and sound electrical design. It serves as a safety tool to the electrical contactors in the implementation of the electrical wiring installations. It serves as a guide to the Building Officials in the issuance of electrical wiring permit and certificate of electrical inspection.
